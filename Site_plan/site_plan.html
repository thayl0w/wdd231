The Place - Site Plan

Site Name

The Place

This name represents a website focused on exploring and showcasing the hidden gems and must-visit spots across the Philippines.

Site Purpose

The site provides comprehensive travel guides and information about the Philippines. It highlights local attractions, food finds, and events, offering expert tips and innovative solutions to enhance travel experiences.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: A tourist planning their first trip to the Philippines wants to find the best attractions and activities.

Scenario 2: A local resident looking for new places to explore and enjoy within the Philippines.

Color Schema

Primary Color: #FF5733 (used for headers and important highlights)

Secondary Color: #33C3FF (used for buttons and links)

Accent Color: #28A745 (used for call-to-action elements)

Typography

Primary Font: Open Sans (used for body text and general content)

Secondary Font: Roboto (used for headings and subheadings)
